This was a large pattern recognition project. I worked with Alexandre Alahi, and we wrote an algorithm to locate a license plate on pictures of cars.

We then extracted the characters on the license plate and used pattern recognition technics to convert the image (group of pixels) into characters (the value of the licence plate).

We worked with Swiss images.
