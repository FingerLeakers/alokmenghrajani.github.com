The intersection of three cylinders (and six half-cylinders) are interesting objects to look at. They are also fun to build out of wood.

The three cylinder case results in a Steinmetz solid.

(image courtesy of Van Helsing)

You can play with a virtual 3d model in openjscad.

The intersection of six cylinders can result in a cube.

You can also play with this case in openjscad.
